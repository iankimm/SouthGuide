Transportation

Bus Taxi Subway KTX
Most used. Cheap

One of the most common transportation in South Korea.
There are many stations around the city with a map.
Each bus will take about 15 minutes to arrive.
Payment : Cash (non higher than 5,000 won) or Card
Check your destination and which bus number you need to take!

Fast. Price varies depending on distance

Taxi can be easily found near the bus stations or streets.
These drivers will take you wherever you want to go.
Payment : Cash or Card
Must avoid traffic hours / Taxi can charge you by driving time

Well organized. Clean

Bad weather? No problem.
These underground torpedo will take you each subway stations fast.
Major cities such as Seoul has many subway stations (almost every 10 blocks).
Payment : transportation card
Must avoid morning time. Students and workers usually use subway.

Fastest way to move around major cities.

Similar to subway stations, but in larger scale.
KTX can cross South Korea within 3 hours.
Very useful when you are traveling far.
Payment : cash or card
Tend to be more expensive.
