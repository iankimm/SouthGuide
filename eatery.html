Hannam Oriental Roast Chicken

Chicken roasted with kimchi and rice inside
located Hannam-dong, Yongsan-gu, Seoul.

    List of food you must try!


  1. Gimbap
    A Korean dish made from cooked rice and ingredients such as vegetables, fish and meats that are rolled in dried sheets of seaweed
    Recommendation : Gimbap Paradise, Teacher Kim, Kobongmin
  2. Gopchang
    Small intestines of cattle with buttery bursts of beefiness paired with a subtly pleasant touch of barnyard in each chewy bite.
    Recommendation : Aunt's Cattle Intestines, Gopchang Story, Gopchang Addcits
  3. McDonalds
    Korean style McDoanlds. Burgers are completely different, flavorful, and delicious.
  4. Bibimbap
    Bibimbap is served as a bowl of warm white rice topped with vegetables or kimchi and gochujang
    Recommendation : Kkot Bape Pida, Otong-Yeong
